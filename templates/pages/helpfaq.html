Help & FAQ

Need more information? Read our FAQ.

General Questions

Is account registration required?

Account registration is not required to search and view listings. It is only required when contacting a seller and when creating your own listing.

We suggest creating an account as it allows you to view and edit your own listings and manage your profile.

How do I edit my listing?

Log in. Click on your name in the navigation bar and go to my listings. There, you can edit or delete your listing.

How do I edit my profile?

Log in. Click on your name in the navigation bar and go to edit profile.

Renting

I need sports equipment right now. How do I find it?

Just go to the homepage and search for the sports equipment you are looking for. If it is currently available, we will display the listing and then you are welcome to contact the lister.

If I searched and there is no equipment available, what should I do?

We might not have equipment listed in your search area yet so try again at a later time. Try broadening your search. Also try clearing filters.

What does subscribe do?

It sends you an email when an equipment becomes available!

Can I request more than one equipment at once?

Yes. In fact, we recommend that Renters request multiple equipment at a time in order to get a quick response. Sometimes, Listers don’t have time to respond right away, so requesting multiple equipment will help you find one when you need it.

What do I do if the Lister doesn't respond?

A big priority for us right now is to create efficient communication between the lister and the person renting. Of course, we realize that things come up. Listers can be on vacation, away from WIFI, or unable to respond due to their regular day job needs. Once again, we recommend that those renting request multiple equipment at a time in order to increase the chances of finding one that works.

Listing

Does it cost me anything to create a listing ?

Nope! You can list as many items as you like, free of charge!

What if I can rent my equipment out certain days?

No problem! Just go on your listings page and mark unavailable! Your post will be hidding from public.

What if my equipments gets damaged while rented?

All damage and personal injury is the responsibility of the Lister during the reservation. ReginasRecreationalRentables is not accountable. Loan equipment at your own risk.

Can I list my equipment for sale on ReginasRecreationalRentables?

Unfortunately, we don’t allow sales on ReginasRecreationalRentables. Currently, we are focused on renting sports equipment.
